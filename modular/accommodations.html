My Accommodations

Disability accommodations on file with BYUI Accessibility Services

These accommodations are officially registered with BYUI Accessibility Services. Contact accessibility@byui.edu or call (208) 496-9210 with any questions.

Testing Accommodations

  • Extra Time 1.50x — 50% extra time on all timed exams and quizzes
  • Interruption for Medication / Food / Drink — can pause tests for medical needs
  • Restroom / Water Breaks During Exams — brief breaks during exams as needed
  • Test Reschedule Option — can reschedule exams due to health flare-ups

  • Food or Water Breaks During Class — as needed
  • Occasional Extensions on Assignments
